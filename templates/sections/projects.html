Projects

  • Remotes.io

    remotes.io Github

    Remotes.io is an open source platform allowing developers to easily add a phone based remote control functionality to their web applications as an alternative to standard input devices like mouse and keyboard. I am one of the two core developers on the project responsible for the vision, architecture and implementation. The project is in its nascency. Remotes.io is already integrated as a plugin in a popular open source presentation framework Reveal.js.

  • Shakido

    Presentation at TC Disrupt Hackathon

    Shakido is like turntable.fm where people can show their appreciation of music by shaking their phones. Every tune playing is given a certain amount of energy that decays with time, unless people shake their phones to provide energy boosts. Each person's shakes are visualized on a screen. The shakes' amplitudes contribute to the energy level of the room. Nobody dances -> no shakes -> next song. Designed, built and shipped in < 24 with my friends Tim, Hycianthe and Matt during TechCrunch Disrupt 2012. Shakido uses SoundCloud API, of course.
